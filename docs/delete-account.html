Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 2026

You can request deletion of your Over It account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, how long it takes, and how to make the request.

The short version: Email us from your account's email address, and we'll delete your account, your conversation history, and everything we've learned about you — permanently, within 30 days.

How to request deletion

Email overitapp.help@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Over It account, with the subject line "Delete my account." We'll verify the request and begin deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, password)
  • Your questionnaire responses from onboarding
  • Your full conversation history with the AI companion
  • Any derived profile data — attachment style, grief stage, and other patterns learned from your conversations

Timeline

We process deletion requests within 30 days of verifying your identity. Once complete, this data cannot be recovered.

Deleting only your conversation history

If you'd like to keep your account but clear your conversation history, you can do this two ways:

  • In the app: tap "New chat" in the top menu. This permanently deletes your conversation history while keeping your account and your Journey insights (attachment style, grief stage, and other patterns we've learned).
  • By email: contact us at overitapp.help@gmail.com and we can delete your conversation history, your Journey/profile data, or both — without deleting your account.
