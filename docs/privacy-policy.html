Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Over It ("we," "our," or "the app") is a companion app designed to support people through breakup grief. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

The short version: We collect what's needed to make Over It work for you — your account details, questionnaire answers, and conversation history. We never sell your data, never use your conversations to train AI models, and never share what you tell us with advertisers.

1. Who we are

Over It is operated as an independent product. If you have questions about this policy, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

2. Important: Over It is not therapy

Over It is an AI companion, not a licensed therapist, counselor, or medical provider. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) or the Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741) immediately.

3. Information we collect

When you use Over It, we collect:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text)
  • Questionnaire responses: answers you provide during onboarding, including relationship details, emotional state, and attachment style questions
  • Conversation data: the messages you exchange with the AI companion
  • Derived profile data: as you chat, our AI identifies patterns — such as grief stage or attachment style — to personalize future responses. This is generated from your conversations, not manually entered
  • Push notification token: if you enable notifications, a device identifier used to send you check-in messages

4. How we use your information

We use your information solely to operate and improve your experience within Over It:

  • To personalize the AI companion's responses based on your history and emotional patterns
  • To maintain your account and conversation history across sessions
  • To send optional check-in notifications, if you've enabled them
We do not use your conversations to train AI models. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your conversations with advertisers.

5. Who processes your data

To operate the app, we use the following service providers, each of which processes data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments:

  • Anthropic — processes your conversation text to generate the AI companion's responses. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests.
  • Upstash (Redis) — stores your account, profile, and conversation data securely.
  • Vercel — hosts our backend infrastructure.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of certain data processing (California residents, under the CCPA)

8. Age requirement

Over It is intended for users 17 years of age and older, given the emotionally mature nature of its content. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. You're talking to an AI

Over It's companion is an artificial intelligence system, not a human. While it's designed to feel warm and personal, it does not have feelings, consciousness, or independent judgment. Please keep this in mind when sharing sensitive information.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encrypted password storage and encrypted data transmission, to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us
